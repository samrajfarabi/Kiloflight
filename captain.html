Speech of Team Captain:

What kind of sorcery could unshackle a certain sector from the shackles of rudiment? To create experts? Sounds easy, right? But to make experts on a sector that is yet fully developed is not that an easy task. In our country the automobile sector is so back footed that we barely have any adequate number of expert engineers,which could be why no one is daring to initiate a fully developed automobile industry in Bangladesh. If we could inspire this generation, ignite that confidence, that they can achieve that feat of building their own car, we might just create history of a new era. This motive is what “Inspired Us” to get ready for this competition.

Arfan Islam
Team Captain
KUET,ME 2k16
