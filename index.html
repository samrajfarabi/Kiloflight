WHO ARE WE!

KILO FLIGHT, the first team from Khulna University of Engineering & Technology, structured with a group of automobile enthusiasts, coming from different regions around Bangladesh. The team has already participated in the most prestigious design competition, FORMULA STUDENT UK 2020, securing a satisfactory position. The team is utterly promising to new challenges and to produce skilled engineers for shaping a vivid future.

More Info

OUR GOAL

The motto of the team is to come up with the optimum solutions to construct a prominent Formula Student racing car. The foremost task is confronting to develop proficient human resources to carry out the automotive future of the country. Carrying the utmost limitations, yet be fulfilled with positivity to go through and pioneering innovation.

More Info

FORMULA STUDENT

Formula Student is the most renowned educational engineering competition, backed by several motorsports industries and high-profile engineers. The competition sees over a hundred of teams over the year, judging the accuracy and refines their knowledge based on real-time engineering practice. The vision is to develop enterprising and innovate young engineers and encourage more young people to pursue their career in engineering.
