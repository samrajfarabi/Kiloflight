KUET FORMULA STUDENT RACING CAR.
DEPARTMENT OF MECHANICAL ENGINEERING.

When we look back about a year ago, the first thing that comes to our mind is “What kept us going this far?” The story behind our story isn’t a smooth one. The road had a whole lot of ups and downs, but mostly downs. The team was first formed in November of the year 2018. Our two founding members sorted the recruited members with a view to attend the fs 2020. They knew that, the only quality of the members that will help the team propagate through the hard way in front was perseverance and dedication. So, he started recruiting the automobile enthusiasts of the campus who are willing to build their career around this sector.The first obstacle faced was inexperience. Since, we are the first team of our university, only a few were there to guide us. We didn’t know where to start. Since the automobile sector is a huge sector, and no one can grasp all the concepts in such short time, the team leaders divided the team into 4 major sectors, 1) Engine, 2) Mechanism, 3)Analysis, 4)Design.

Now we do have more way to go but we are hopeful we will reach our destination .We are expected to attend FORMULA STUDENT in August , 2020 . FS is consisted of 3 events , 1) Combustion Vehicle (CV) 2) Electric Vehicle (EV) 3) Driverless Vehicle (DV). If things go as planned we will participate in the Combustion Vehicle .When we think of all that help we got , people that funded us ,the hark works we have done and the expectations that people have on us , failure is not an option . That thought kept us going all these time .We believe our hard work will pay off.
